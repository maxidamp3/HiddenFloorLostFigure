ABOUT

Greetings and a most sincere welcome to the haunting and melancholic world of HIDDEN FLOOR: LOST FIGURE. This project is a labor of love, a deep dive into themes of grief, guilt, and the fragile specters of memory, all presented through the visceral medium of rhythm and music.

HIDDEN FLOOR: LOST FIGURE is a fan-made expansion and re-imagining, built upon the foundation of the acclaimed mod, HIDDEN FLOOR: NO FEAR. While it draws inspiration from its predecessor, it carves its own distinct path, introducing a new narrative arc and a unique aesthetic atmosphere.

This modification is a character-driven experience, featuring a cast of original personas, each meticulously designed with their own visual identity and deeply integrated lore. The story you are about to witness is a personal and harrowing one, exploring the psychological aftermath of loss and the ways in which we are haunted not by ghosts, but by our own perceived failures.

The journey is structured across several songs, each one a new chapter in this poignant and emotional tale. We hope you are prepared to step into the shadows and uncover what was lost.

OG

This project is inspired by the original HIDDEN FLOOR: NO FEAR mod, created by talented developers in the gaming community. The original mod received widespread acclaim for its innovative approach to horror aesthetics and unique atmospheric design.

We took the core concepts and expanded on them, adding new characters, story, and gameplay elements, while maintaining the distinctive dark aesthetic that made the original so memorable.

The original HIDDEN FLOOR: NO FEAR was renowned for its creepy elements and unpredictable encounters. We maintained these core principles while adding new characters, new backgrounds, and deeper character backstories.

We also added new mechanics that complement the existing ones. The result is a gameplay experience that is both familiar to veterans and accessible to new players discovering the HIDDEN FLOOR universe for the first time.

This fan project is our tribute to the incredible work of the original game's developers, and we hope it brings the same sense of mystery and tension that made us fall in love with HIDDEN FLOOR: NO FEAR.
